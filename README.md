## About

*The Ruin and the Road* is a short readers theater piece for synagogue use on Tisha b'Av. It retells the Talmudic story of Rabbi Yosi, who enters a ruin of Jerusalem to pray and encounters Elijah — through a contemporary character named Mara, who returns to a sacred place that once hurt her.

The play weaves together three themes:
- **D'veikut** (Devotion) — cleaving, holding fast
- **Emunah** (Faith) — steadiness, trust
- **Tikvah** (Hope) — a thread held taut

The booklet includes the Talmudic source text in Hebrew and English, notes on the source, a note on the character of Mara, a Tisha b'Av ritual guide, the full readers theater script, symbolic commentary, a musical arrangement guide, and discussion prompts.

## Printing the Booklet

The HTML and PDF are formatted for **saddle-stitch booklet printing** on standard Letter paper:

1. Open `index.html` in a browser (or use the PDF)
2. File → Print
3. Paper: **Letter (8.5×11)**, Orientation: **Landscape**
4. Two-sided: **On**, Binding: **Short-Edge**
5. Stack the 4 printed sheets, fold in half, staple along the center fold

If the back side prints rotated 180°, switch to **Long-Edge** binding.
